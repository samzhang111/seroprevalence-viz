Prevalence calculator (lab calibration data)

This calculator is one of multiple resources available through the COVID-19 Testing Group. It accompanies the manuscript "Estimating SARS-CoV-2 seroprevalence and epidemiological parameters with uncertainty from serological surveys", which is available as a preprint. This work is not yet peer reviewed.
Given test results in a population sample, and the test's lab calibration data, what is the posterior distribution of prevalence in the population, test sensitivity, and test specificity?
